Data Analysis and Visualization of Yorku Students Commutes

EECS 6414: Case Study Project

Project Motivation

Importance

  • Scheduling
  • Emergency?
  • Event planning
  • Interruptions

Potential applications

  1. Demographics for YorkU students
  2. Transportation modeling for YorkU students
  3. Predictive analysis modeling for future changes and emergencies

Proposed Results

Demography of YorkU students/employees

  • Location, distance
  • Distributions of majors, year of entry/graduation

Transportation modeling

  1. Average commute time and cost, most used transit
  2. Analysis for specific areas

Predictive analysis modeling

  • Demographically. “which area will gain more commuting pressure?”
  • Changes in routes. “If an existing route is canceled or new route is created, how does it influence the YorkU students commuting time ”
  • Bad weather impact / Service outage impact
